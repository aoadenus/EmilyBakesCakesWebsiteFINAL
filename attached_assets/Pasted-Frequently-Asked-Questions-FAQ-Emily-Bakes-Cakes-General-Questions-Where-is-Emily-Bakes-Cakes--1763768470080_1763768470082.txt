Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

(Emily Bakes Cakes)

General Questions
Where is Emily Bakes Cakes located?

We are located near the Galleria area in Houston, Texas. Our storefront is open for walk-in purchases of standard cakes, cupcakes, cookies, pastries, and seasonal items.

Do you offer walk-in items?

Yes. Our standard products—including cakes, cupcakes, cookies, pastries, and other baked goods—are always available for walk-in customers.

Do you sell ready-made cake mixes?

No. All of our cakes and pastries are baked from scratch using Emily’s family recipes combined with her European baking training. We never use store-bought or ready-made mixes.

Custom Cake Orders
Can I place a custom cake order online?

No. Custom cake orders cannot be placed online. Customers may call, email, or visit the store, and our staff will enter the order into our internal order system.

How early should I place a custom cake order?

Custom cakes should be ordered at least 2 days in advance. Exceptions may be granted at the Store Manager’s discretion.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes. A 50% deposit is required for all custom orders.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, debit cards, and all major credit cards.

Can I customize any cake?

Yes. All of our standard cakes can be customized with your preferred icing, filling, writing, and decorations. Cupcakes can also be customized (cupcake fillings are applied in the center top portion).

Do you accept inspiration photos?

Yes. Customers may bring inspiration photos, clippings, screenshots, or sample images. Our decorators will evaluate feasibility and make the final decision on what can be replicated safely and accurately.

How many fillings can a cake layer have?

Each layer may have no filling, one filling, or at most two layers of the same filling. Mixed fillings within a single layer are not offered.

When will my custom cake be ready?

All custom cakes are completed and fully decorated at least four hours before the scheduled pickup time.

Who approves the final cake?

Every custom cake is approved by either Emily or the Bakery Manager before it is released to the customer.

Cancellations & Changes
Can I cancel my custom order?

Custom orders may be canceled before baking begins.

Can I change my design?

Design changes may be made before decorating begins. However, changes could affect the final price.

Pricing & Products
Do you have a price list?

Yes. Cake sizes range from 6-inch rounds to full-sheet cakes, with prices starting at $20 and going up to $200 depending on size.

Do you offer discounts?

Some “Preferred” customers may receive a 10% discount, granted at the discretion of Emily or the Store Manager.

What flavors do you offer?

We offer a full range of cake, icing, and filling flavors including:
• Cake: vanilla, almond, yellow, chocolate, strawberry, and more
• Filling: buttercreams, curds, fruit fillings, mousses, praline, and more
• Icing: buttercreams, cream cheese, and chocolate ganache
Full lists are available on our product pages.

What decorations can I choose from?

We offer a wide variety of decorations including buttercream flowers, fondant, silk flowers, sugar photos, toys, themed figures, ribbons, sports toppers, and seasonal designs.

Customer Data & Loyalty
Do you keep customer history?

Yes. Our system securely stores customer preferences and past orders to help us better recommend flavors and designs for future events. (This feature is part of our new internal order system.)

Website & Online Experience
Is your website mobile-friendly?

Our new site is designed to give customers clear information about our products, flavors, and order process, with improved functionality across devices. (The original site had limited mobile support, which we have actively improved.)

Still Have Questions?

Call us, email us, or stop by the bakery — we’re always happy to help make your celebration sweet!
